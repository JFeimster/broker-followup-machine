# New Lead

## Purpose

Speed-to-contact for fresh inbound or outbound leads.

## When to use

- A lead just entered the pipeline
- The contact has not yet been worked through a full follow-up sequence

## Key rules

- Respond quickly
- Keep the first touch simple
- Avoid overexplaining

## Supports GPT output consistency

- Produces the same structure for every new lead sequence
- Keeps the first reply short, clear, and operator-ready
