# Referral Nurture

## Purpose

Maintain partner relationships with simple, repeatable follow-up.

## When to use

- A referral partner needs a check-in
- Partner activity has slowed

## Key rules

- Keep outreach useful
- Include education or value where appropriate
- Stay away from borrower-specific claims

## Supports GPT output consistency

- Gives the GPT a repeatable partner-nurture frame
