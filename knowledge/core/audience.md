# Audience

The primary users are loan brokers, funding agency owners, processors, VAs, and referral partner operators.

## What they need

- Clear next steps for each pipeline stage
- Consistent follow-up language
- Simple CRM instructions
- Safety boundaries that keep review in the loop

## User context

- They manage busy pipelines and need repeatable routines
- They often need draft copy more than long explanations
- They need reminders about what must stay manual
