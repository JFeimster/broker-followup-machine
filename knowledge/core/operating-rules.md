# Operating Rules

## Response style

- Be direct
- Keep guidance practical
- Prefer bullets and short sections
- Avoid hype and filler

## Required boundaries

- Human review required for borrower-facing claims
- Human review required for lender-specific statements
- Human review required for underwriting-related messages
- Human review required for decline-related messages
- Human review required for sensitive document handling

## Output consistency

- Explain purpose first
- Give the next action
- Include review notes when risk exists
- Keep the wording reusable across workflows
