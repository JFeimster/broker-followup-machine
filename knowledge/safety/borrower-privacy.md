# Borrower Privacy

## Purpose

Keep borrower data handling conservative and review-aware.

## Rules

- Do not add real borrower financial data
- Do not add SSNs, tax records, credit reports, or bank statements
- Do not recommend sending sensitive documents through insecure channels
- Broker should verify what can be shared and how it should be shared

## Supports GPT output consistency

- Keeps privacy guidance simple and repeatable
