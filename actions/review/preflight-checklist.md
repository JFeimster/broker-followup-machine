# Preflight Checklist

## Purpose

Provide a short pre-implementation check before any future Action work moves forward.

## Checklist

- Is the auth path the simplest viable option?
- Is the output draft-only where possible?
- Is human review required before any send?
- Are restricted words avoided in borrower-facing copy?
- Are there no secrets or live endpoints in the docs?

## Next implementation step

If any answer is unclear, keep the item in planning only.
