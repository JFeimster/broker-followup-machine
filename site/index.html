Workflow categories

What the GPT is built to handle

The site focuses on operational sequences that keep deals moving without drifting into hype or unsafe borrower claims.

New lead follow-up

Capture the first response window, set next steps, and reduce dropped handoffs.

Missing document follow-up

Keep requests organized so document collection stays specific and trackable.

Stalled deal revival

Reopen dead air with disciplined follow-up instead of repeated manual chasing.

Declined borrower reactivation

Use careful re-entry messaging and human review before any sensitive wording goes out.

Funded client renewals

Track return touchpoints and keep the post-fund relationship organized.

Referral partner nurture

Support repeat business from accountants, realtors, and other partner channels.

Dead lead revival

Bring old records back into motion with clear timing and controlled messaging.

Safety preview

Protect the message before it reaches the borrower

Human review is required anywhere the language can affect borrower trust, legal exposure, or lender-specific messaging.

Review before sending

  • Finance details or lender-specific statements
  • Decline-related wording or underwriting-related notes
  • Sensitive document references or file handling
  • Any borrower-facing claim about outcomes or certainty

Keep the tone practical

  • Use direct follow-up language
  • Prefer the next action over broad promises
  • Write for reviewable workflow steps
  • Keep the operator in control of the final send
