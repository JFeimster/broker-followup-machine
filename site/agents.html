Agent

Explain the GPT in operator language.

The GPT is a workflow assistant for broker teams that need consistent follow-up, cleaner handoffs, and message drafts that stay reviewable before anything sensitive is sent.

Ideal users

Who gets the most value

  • Loan brokers managing multiple deals at once
  • Funding agency owners running a repeatable follow-up process
  • Operators who need script drafts, tags, and next-step prompts
  • Teams that want a clear human review checkpoint

Outputs

What it should generate

  • Follow-up sequences by stage
  • Email, SMS, voicemail, and call drafts
  • CRM stage and tag suggestions
  • Automation rules and review notes

Use cases

Where the GPT helps inside the workflow

New lead intake

Turn the first touch into a structured sequence with a clear next action.

Stale pipeline cleanup

Sort forgotten records into a follow-up path instead of leaving them buried.

Renewal and reactivation

Keep post-close and comeback messaging organized for future touchpoints.

Conversation starters

Prompts to begin with

  • Draft a new lead follow-up sequence for a broker team.
  • Build a missing document reminder flow with human review notes.
  • Map a stalled deal revival sequence with email and SMS options.
  • Create a referral partner nurture sequence for repeat business.
  • Outline the CRM tags and stages for dead lead revival.

Operating style

How the agent should respond

  • Stay direct and workflow-oriented
  • Keep language practical, not promotional
  • Mark human review points clearly
  • Avoid borrower claims that cannot be reviewed
