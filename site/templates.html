Templates

Reusable formats for the main communication channels.

Templates should give the operator a starting point without locking the language into one tone or one scenario.

Email

Short, clear messages for sequence-based follow-up and recap.

SMS

Brief check-ins that support fast response and easy review.

Calls

Call opening notes and next-step prompts for live conversations.

Voicemail

Concise callbacks that state the reason for contact and the next action.

CRM tasks

Task language that keeps ownership, timing, and status clear.

Objections

Response patterns for common pushback without overpromising.

Follow-up calendars

Recurring touchpoint plans that are easy to maintain.

Format rules

What each template should do

  • State the purpose in the first line
  • Keep the ask specific
  • Leave room for human review
  • Stay neutral and operational

Reuse pattern

How to adapt templates

  • Swap the scenario, keep the structure
  • Adjust channel length without changing the point
  • Keep review notes close to the draft
  • Match the operator's preferred voice
