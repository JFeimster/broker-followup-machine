## 6. Safety / Compliance-Conscious Section

### Headline

Built for Broker-Safe Follow-Up

### Copy

Broker Follow-Up Machine GPT is designed to move conversations forward without making risky funding claims. It avoids approval promises, eligibility claims, guaranteed outcomes, lender-specific assumptions, and automated underwriting-style language.

### Safe Language Examples

- “This looks worth reviewing.”
- “We can take a closer look.”
- “A broker should review this.”
- “The next step is to complete your file.”
- “It may be worth revisiting if your situation has changed.”

### Avoided Language

- “Guaranteed approval”
- “You qualify”
- “You are approved”
- “We can definitely get you funded”
- “This lender will approve you”

### Important Note

This GPT does not provide legal, tax, accounting, underwriting, lending compliance, credit decisioning, or lender-specific advice. Broker review is recommended before sending sensitive, deal-specific, terms-related, decline-related, or lender-specific communication.

### Follow-Up System: Missing Document Follow-Up

Collect required documents clearly so the borrower file can move to broker review.

Borrower submitted application but still owes documents.
